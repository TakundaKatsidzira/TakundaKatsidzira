# Gameplay Rules

## Overview

Sequence is played on a 10x10 board where players place tokens corresponding to cards from their hands.

## Objective

Create a sequence of 5 tokens in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win.

## Special Rules

- Corner squares act as free spaces usable by both players.
- One-eyed Jacks remove an opponent's token.
- Two-eyed Jacks act as wild cards.

## Turn Flow

- Players alternate turns.
- On each turn, a player places a token on a board position matching a card in their hand.
- Players draw cards after playing.
